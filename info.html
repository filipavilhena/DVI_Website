Context

This platform was developed in the scope of the master's degree project "Participation in Dynamic Visual Identities".
The dissertation focuses on Dynamic Visual Identities (DVI's) as its main research area, with a particular emphasis on participation and it aimed to investigate participatory practices in Visual Identity Systems, as well as the participation's particular characteristics.

This platform's goal is to disseminate this knowledge and make it directly accessible to design practice. It brings together the identities and related information analysed and gathered during the research, allowing the developed work's exploration, contributing to the creation of new Participatory Visual Identities and to the ongoing discussion related to participation and dynamism in design.
