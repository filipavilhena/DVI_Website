Filters


Who participates? - The group of people who influences the identity through participation.



How many people? - How many people influence a single variation of the participatory elements.



Is it voluntary? - If people's participation is voluntary or involuntary.



Is it intended? - If the participation was originally intended when developing the identity system or if it emerged from the system's existing structure.



When? - In what part of the design process the participation occurs.



Where? - Where do people have the opportunity to participate and engage with the identity.



How? - How people can interact with the elements of an identity system.



What's the medium? - What type of medium allows the participation to happen.



What's the user input? - What kind of input users contribute with in order to participate.



What changes? - What visual characteristics change on the visual identity because of the participation.



What's the target? - What are the system's elements that support participation.



Where are the elements integrated into? - If the participatory elements are part of the visual identity system or of a system's participatory extension.



What are the output applications? - What practical applications are a product of the participation's results.



Who are the applications accessible to? - What group of people is able to see the participation's applications.
